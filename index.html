About me!

My name is Norette Ingabire and I am from Burundi.
I am currently a third year student at Dartmouth College, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Global Health.
I am passionate about technology and the ways it can be applied in solving daily problems. I am interested in working at the intersection of the healthcare and technology.
Over the summer, I got to work on mobile application development and really enjoyed it. While it was fun to play around with the UI design/colors, it was amazing to realize how different choices bring a unique ambience to the application. I am enthusiastic about joining DALI because it is a space that brings together many talented students and allows them to enhance their skillset while working on impactful projects.
In my free time, I enjoy dancing and reading(fiction is my favorite)!
